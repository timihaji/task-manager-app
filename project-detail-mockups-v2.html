Project detail v2 — six dense approaches

Combining the kanban of 01 Trello-faithful, the brief/decisions of 02 Notion-doc, and the KPIs/charts of 05 Dashboard-first — at the information density of 03 Linear-dense. Same content in all six; what differs is the organizational philosophy.

Common ingredients across all six: kanban (To do / Doing / Done), brief + outcome statement + decisions, KPI cluster (% done, days left, blocked, overdue, status, health), burndown sparkline, dense subtask data.

01Triptych
3-paneeverything visibledensity: high

Tradeoff: Brief, board, and metrics each get their own permanent pane. Maximum simultaneous visibility — but each pane is narrow, so cards are small and the brief gets a column not a page.

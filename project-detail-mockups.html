Project detail view — six approaches

Six distinct takes on what opens when you click into a project. They vary on layout, density, and tone — each makes a different tradeoff. Read the label, scan the mockup, see which one matches how you'd actually use it.

01Trello-faithful

Tradeoff: Pure execution mode — board fills the screen, brief and metrics live behind a button. You're here to push cards, not think.
